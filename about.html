Our History

St. Mary's Boarding Secondary School Kitende (SMASK) was founded in 2001 by Dr. Lawrence Mulindwa with a vision to provide world-class education and promote sports excellence in Uganda. Located along the Entebbe-Kampala Expressway in Kitende, Wakiso District, the school quickly established itself as a leading institution for both academic and co-curricular development. Inspired by a commitment to holistic education, SMASK has grown into a mixed boarding school offering Ordinary (O) and Advanced (A) level programs, serving students from Uganda and beyond, including Rwanda, Kenya, and Tanzania. Over the years, it has earned a reputation for academic distinction, consistently ranking among the top performers in national examinations, and for sports prowess, notably as the home of Vipers SC. Today, St. Mary's Kitende stands as a beacon of opportunity, shaping principled and talented individuals for a global impact.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative education that nurtures academic excellence, sports talent, and moral integrity in a supportive Christian environment, empowering students to become innovative leaders and responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be the best centre of holistic learning at secondary education with a global impact.
To be a premier institution of learning and character development, producing globally competitive individuals through excellence in education, sports, and innovation.

Our Core Values

  1. Excellence - Striving for the highest standards in academics, sports, and personal growth.
  2. Integrity - Upholding honesty, accountability, and ethical behavior in all endeavors.
  3. Discipline - Fostering self-control, respect, and responsibility among students and staff.
  4. Innovation - Encouraging creativity and critical thinking to solve modern challenges.
  5. Community - Building a spirit of teamwork, service, and unity within and beyond the school.
  6. Faith - Grounding our actions in Christian principles and moral values.
