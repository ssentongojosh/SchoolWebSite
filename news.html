OPENING DATE FOR TERM 1

The first term of the 2025 academic year will commence on January 12th 2025 with all students expect to report having completed at least 70% of the school fees.

BEGINING OF TERM EXAMS

Beginning of term exams will commence on January 27th 2025. Students are argued to do thorough revision for these exams as they contribute 25% to the final mark.

PARENTS-TEACHERS MEETING

The parents-teahers meeting will take place on February 21st 2025.

Parents will have a meeting with the students and the teachers to assess the students' performance and share ideas on how best the performance can be improved.

MID-TERM EXAMS

Mid-term exams will commence on February 24th 2025. This set will also contribute 25% to the final mark.

MUSIC DANCE AND DRAMA DATE


The school will have MDD competitions that will see the different houses compete for a bull and the best students getting activities.

The competitions will take place on 14th and 15th March 2025.

All parents are invited to watch what the students can do outside books.

END OF TERM EXAMS

End of term exams will commence on 31st March 2025. This set will contribute 50% to the final mark.

CLOSING DATE FOR TERM 1

The first term of the 2025 academic year will end on 11th April 2025
